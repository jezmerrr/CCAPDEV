About the Platform

The DLSU Computer Lab Reservation platform is a web-based application designed to streamline the process of booking computer lab seats. Built for DLSU students and lab technicians, this system makes it easy to check availability, reserve slots, and manage lab access efficiently. Whether you need a quiet space to work on assignments or want to ensure you have a computer ready when you arrive, our platform takes the hassle out of lab reservations.

Key Features

Real-Time Availability

View current seat availability across all computer labs without needing to refresh the page.

Flexible Reservations

Book slots in 30-minute intervals up to 7 days in advance. Reserve multiple slots at once under a single reservation.

Privacy Options

Choose to reserve anonymously or display your name publicly.

Easy Management

Edit or cancel your reservations anytime from your dashboard.
