Find available slots and reserve your seat across DLSU's computer labs in just a few clicks.

About the Platform

The DLSU Computer Lab Reservation platform is a web-based system developed to make reserving computer lab seats more efficient and convenient. It allows students to check available slots, book seats, and manage their reservations in a simple and organized way. Created for both students and lab technicians, the platform helps improve how lab usage is managed while reducing confusion and manual processes.

Key Features

Real-Time Availability

View current seat availability across all computer labs without needing to refresh the page.

Flexible Reservations

Book slots in 30-minute intervals up to 7 days in advance. Reserve multiple slots at once under a single reservation.

Privacy Options

Choose to reserve anonymously or display your name publicly.

Easy Management

Edit or cancel your reservations anytime from your dashboard.
